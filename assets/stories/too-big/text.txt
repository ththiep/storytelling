Too Big! Too Small! Shanu is too big for Ammi to carry to the market. She stretches her arms and laughs when Ammi says she is growing every day. But Shanu is also too small to walk to school alone on the busy road. Every morning Shanu asks the same question. Am I too small to read this story by myself? Or am I too big to sit in Ammi's lap and listen? Her brother smiles and says she is both at once. So Shanu opens the book carefully and finds out. She can read some words on her own, and she can share the rest with someone she loves. Today she is just the right size for a wonderful story.
